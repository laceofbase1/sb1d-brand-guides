Brand essence

A smarter, faster, debt-free path to a degree.

Smarter by 1 Degree helps students earn a high school diploma and an accredited bachelor's degree at the same time, for up to 80% less and 4+ years faster. The brand stands for one idea: a small, strategic shift in direction changes where a student ends up.

Brand pillars
  • Credible — accredited, advised, real degrees.
  • Strategic — a planned path, not busywork.
  • Empowering — students drive their future.
  • Efficient — less time, less debt, more direction.
The logo

The graduation mark

The mortarboard cap resting on a confident S is the heart of the identity. It signals the destination, a degree, in a single glance. Protect it.

Primary mark
Horizontal lockup
On navy (white field)

Do

  • Keep clear space equal to the cap's height on all sides.
  • On dark or busy backgrounds, place the logo on a white field.
  • Pair the mark with the WASC seal in trust-building contexts.

Don't

  • Recolor or redraw the mark; keep the navy, white, and black as drawn.
  • Stretch, rotate, or add effects to the logo.
  • Place the full-color mark on a low-contrast background.
