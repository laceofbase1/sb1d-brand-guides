The logo

The logo, untouched

Your logo is the anchor of trust, so it never changes. Momentum lives everywhere around it. Keep clear space, keep contrast, and on dark or busy fields set the full-color mark on a clean white field.

03
Typography

Archivo, with Hanken Grotesk

Archivo is the voice of the wordmark: heavy, geometric, confident, used for every headline and label. Hanken Grotesk is its warmer counterpart for body and UI, friendly enough for a teenager, clear enough for a parent.

04
The momentum system

Motion that means progress

Momentum is not decoration, it is the brand's one rule made visible: every student is moving forward. Motion always travels the same way, ahead, and always eases out, never bounces.

Progress, made visible

Show how far a student has come. Progress tracks fill navy-to-azure, left to right.

Forward cues

The azure arrow is the brand's gesture. On hover it nudges forward, never back.

The rules of motion

One language across web, video, and UI.

Duration 180ms UI · 600ms reveals
Easing cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)
Direction left → right, up. Never bounce.
