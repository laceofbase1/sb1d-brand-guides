New Direction 04 · Premium / Academic

A Degree Ahead

A calm, premium identity for the school's real promise: students finish years ahead with an accredited bachelor's degree already earned, and no debt behind it. The look of a trusted advisor, not an online-school ad.

The big idea

Don't just graduate. Graduate a whole degree ahead.

Most students finish high school and then start college. Smarter by 1 Degree students finish high school already holding college credit, or a full bachelor's, debt-free and years sooner. This direction treats that head start as something earned and honored: the laurel, the gold, the quiet confidence of a real credential.

The feeling

Considered. Optimistic. Grown-up. This is the trusted advisor your family already wishes they had, not a sales pitch.

The logo

Your mark, kept whole

The logo is yours and it doesn't change. In this system it sits on bone or a clean white field, never redrawn or recolored. Pine and gold are the world around it, the colors of growth and of a real degree earned.

On bone
On white
On pine (white field)

Do

  • Keep the logo full-color on bone or a clean white field.
  • Let gold mark achievement: a key word, a rule, a button.
  • Surround the mark with generous, quiet space.

Don't

  • Redraw, recolor, or flatten the artwork to one tone.
  • Set the full-color mark straight onto pine; use a white field.
  • Crowd the mark with texture or photography.
Color

Pine, gold, and bone

Strategy: committed. Deep pine signals growth and seriousness; warm gold marks the achievement of a real degree; bone keeps everything breathing.
